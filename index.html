Resilient agriculture - Contemporary farming methods mixed with agroecology and novel farming technology

Climate resilient agriculture is able to survive and thrive over shocks and disturbances caused by climate change, such as severe storms, heat waves and droughts. Without resilience farmers risk losing their crops, livestock and their livelihood For smallholder farmers, these shocks can exhaust scarce resources of cash, seeds and livestock.

Agriculture technology

We share information on contemporary technologies available for agriculture. These technologies include farmland irrigation, water capture and storage, hydroponics and renewable energy for agriculture and domestic use. We can help local farmers to acquire and finance farming technologies by providing contacts and funding.
